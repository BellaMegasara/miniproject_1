About Us

Nestled in the heart of Fremantle, Western Australia, our beautiful 18-hole golf course offers the perfect blend of challenge and accessibility for golfers of all skill levels. Known for its rolling fairways and strategically placed obstacles, our course provides an exhilarating experience for seasoned golfers while remaining friendly and approachable for beginners. The lush greens and stunning coastal views highlight the natural beauty of Fremantle, making every round both enjoyable and rewarding.

Whether you're looking to refine your skills or simply unwind with a relaxing game, our course promises a memorable golfing experience set against the backdrop of a vibrant and historic locale.
